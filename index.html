🐻 Hello !

📷 Loïse Doyen
Hello ! I'm Grizzly Cogs. I make electronic and chiptune music, and original soundtrack for games since 2015. Sometimes it is chill, sometimes it is catchy, sometimes it is melancholic, but it is always about feelings and nostalgia.

I released 4 albums and some singles. I also made soundtracks for some web or mobile indie games. From time to time I like to play my music live. I travelled in 7 countries and play ~30 gigs.

Here is a website where you can find all my work. Have fun 😃

🎹 Run! Run! Jump! Punch!

📷 Loïse Doyen
Run! Run! Jump! Punch! is a Chiptune / Punk rock band from Lille, France in which I play Synth, and drum-programming.

Born from a collision between Punk-rock and lo-fi/chiptune electronic music, Run! Run! Jump! Punch! delivers happiness where soft synth melodies melt with catchy and energetic saturated guitars. Add a pinch of football-like chorus voices, and you will get all the ingredients of their music, to listen to on records or to sweat in live !
